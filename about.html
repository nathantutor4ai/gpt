    My Journey into AI Education

    From software engineer to AI specialist to passionate educator

    The Beginning

    My journey started as a traditional software engineer, but I quickly realized that AI was transforming every industry. I spent countless hours learning machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing. What struck me most was how difficult it was to find practical, career-focused AI education.

    The Breakthrough

    After landing my first AI engineering role at a Fortune 500 company, I started mentoring colleagues who wanted to transition into AI. I discovered that most people didn't need PhD-level theory they needed practical skills that could immediately impact their careers. This realization changed everything.

    The Mission

    Today, I'm dedicated to democratizing AI education. My mission is simple: help ambitious professionals gain practical AI skills that advance their careers. Whether you're looking to transition into AI, enhance your current role, or start your own AI-powered business, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.
